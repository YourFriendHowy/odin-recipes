Lasagna

Description

A simple recipe for a wonderful lasagna dish. This dish is super easy to make at home and only takes a few ingredients.
Im really not sure what else to put here im no chef.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Parmesan
  • mozzarella
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Optional: Garlic bread

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Bring water to a boil while you cook the meat, be sure to flavor the meat with salt and pepper to taste
  3. Add the noodles to the water once it is boiling
  4. Once the meat is cooked, go ahead and drain the fat and add the pasta sauce
  5. Once the sauce is boiling let it simmer while you drain the noodles.
  6. While the sauce simmers rinse the noodles and allow them to dry for a bit
  7. Once all ingredients are ready and the sauce has simmered for 5-10 minutes cover the bottom of your dish with sauce
  8. From here layer sauce, parmesan, noodles, until all the sauce or noodles are gone.
  9. Cover the top of the dish generously with parmesan or mozzarella cheese then place in the oven for 1 hour
  10. Don't forget to add the garlic bread after you take the lasagna out to sit!
