BBQ Chicken

Description

A savory and bold barbeque chicken.

Ingredients

  • bbq sauce
  • brown sugar
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • A1 sauce
  • mustard
  • grape jelly
  • whiskey
  • honey
  • peppercorn medley
  • seasoned salt
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • black pepper
  • mustard (for binder)
  • boneless skinless chicken breasts (or your preferred variety)

Steps

  1. start with the barbecue sauce in a saucepan over medium heat
  2. add Worcestershire sauce, A1 sauce, mustard, grape jelly, and whiskey
  3. stir to combine and let it warm through
  4. add brown sugar and honey gradually, tasting as you go to balance the tanginess
  5. simmer the sauce on low heat, stirring occasionally until it thickens slightly
  6. prepare the chicken by patting it dry
  7. rub a thin layer of mustard over the chicken as a binder
  8. season with peppercorn medley, seasoned salt, garlic powder, onion powder, and black pepper
  9. let the chicken rest while the sauce continues to simmer
  10. grill or cook the chicken as desired, basting with the sauce during the last few minutes of cooking
  11. serve with additional sauce on the side if desired
